About Us

Office Impact is a woman-owned, national distributor of office products.

Shipping from a network of 40 fully stocking warehouses across the continental U.S., we serve most major metropolitan areas.

With 25,000 stocking SKU’s in each location and using nearby distribution centers for product backup, our order fill rates are some of the highest in the industry.

High product fill rates reduce backorders and delays.

Free next day delivery is a standard in our coverage areas.

Free SAME DAY delivery is also available where possible.

With 22 years of experience in the industry we understand that while our major competitors focus primarily on price they miss the mark on the sometimes more important aspects of doing business.

At Office Impact our customers have come to expect the following:

  1. Full range of brand name and compatible products at competitive prices
  2. Next day FREE delivery*
  3. Same day FREE delivery when needed*
  4. Prompt and intelligent answers to product questions, inquiries and hard to find items.
  5. No hassle returns

* Subject to location and availability

That's how we impact your business.
